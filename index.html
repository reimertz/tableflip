Definition of table-flipping

"Event characterized by a melodramatic gesture at the explosive end of extreme frustration, anger or resignation in which a hothead literally flips a table. Used to indicate that the flipper is ill-equipped to continue rationally, has lost his/her cool, and will no linger sit at, or come to, said table."

tableflip makes a homepage flippable

and lets angry visitors (╯°□°)╯ ︵ ┻━┻

if something is broken.

But a (╯°□°)╯ ︵ ┻━┻ would be

kind of useless without feedback

so tableflip will ask for it.

Documentation

The tableflip-button is positioned by top, bottom, left and right and comes in small, medium or large sizes.

The tableflip-form is customizable by a personal message and feedback will be sent to an email of your choosing.

That give you the following API:

position

bottom | top | left | right

size

small | medium | large

key

freeforall

email

anemailthatwill@receivefeedback.com

message

I'm sorry you flipped out and that I broke your heart.

Usage

tableflip is self-initiating when you interact with the API by adding attributes to the script tag.

tableflip could also be initiated later by calling tableFlip.init()
